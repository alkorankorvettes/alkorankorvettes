Who We Are

A Parade Unit With a Purpose

The Korvettes are a driving unit of the Al Koran Shriners. We wheel our custom orange mini-cars through parades and community events, drawing smiles and turning attention toward the mission every Shriner shares: helping kids.

Every wave and every lap is in service of Shriners International and its network of children's hospitals. Any Shriner in good standing with the Al Koran Shriners is welcome to join the unit.

To learn more about becoming a Shriner and the path to membership, visit beashrinernow.com — we would be honored to welcome you.
